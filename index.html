Our Story

A Coastal Haven in Ratnagiri

Experience the Soul of Konkan

Nestled along the pristine shores of Ratnagiri, Shashi Vishwa is a premium beach homestay that blends modern luxury with the timeless charm of India's Konkan coast. Wake up to the melody of waves, dine on authentic Konkani seafood, and let the golden sunsets paint your evenings.

Located near the iconic Nachne Road, our homestay offers easy access to Ratnagiri's most celebrated attractions — from the sacred Ganpatipule Beach to the historic Jaigad Fort and the serene Bhatye Beach.

Nearby Attractions

Ganpatipule Beach

A spectacular and pristine beach known for its silver sands, clear waters, and the 400-year-old Swayambhu Ganpati Temple located right on the shore.

Bhatye Beach

A serene, black sand beach fringed with casuarina trees. Perfect for morning walks, sunset viewing, and enjoying peaceful coastal vibes.

Jaigad Fort

A 16th-century coastal fortification nestled on a cliff overlooking the Sangameshwar river meeting the Arabian Sea.

Jai Vinayak Temple

A beautifully constructed modern temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, featuring distinct coastal architecture and serene gardens.

Ratnadurg Fort

Surrounded by the Arabian Sea on three sides, this historic fort offers breathtaking panoramic views of the ocean.
